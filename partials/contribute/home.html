Contribute

Reciprocity is a key principle underlying conservative systems. In that spirit, let's open a two-way, constructive dialogue. At this time, the best way to contribute is through constructive feedback and collaboration.

Some suggested reasons for contacting us:

  • Want to feature SPConservation on your blog / invite us to speak at your event?
  • Want SPConservation to present our work at your event?
  • Want to collaborate on some aspect of the website?
  • Want to use a conservative optimizer in your work/research?
  • Writing a paper and want to use/cite a conservative optimizer?
  • Need help setting up a distributed system for conservative optimization?
  • Is there a specific optimization problem you'd like us to add to the examples library?
  • Does some content need a deeper or more intuitive explanation?

There are many interesting and important research questions to be addressed. Want to collaborate with us to start answering them?
